Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2023

1. Information We Collect

When you use our EV Savings Calculator, we may collect:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous interaction data (slider positions, calculation results)
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device type (for analytics)
  • Voluntary Information: Email/name if you contact us via forms

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the calculator's accuracy
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Respond to customer inquiries
  • Prevent abuse of our services

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We:

  • Use HTTPS encryption for all data transmissions
  • Store minimal logs (retained for 30 days)
  • Implement industry-standard security measures

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Remember your fuel type preference
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics (anonymized data)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • Fuel Price APIs: To provide real-time petrol/diesel rates
  • Hosting Provider: Vercel/Netlify (server logs)

These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Ask for corrections or deletion
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

Contact us at privacy@jaipurevchargers.com to exercise these rights.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of significant changes via:

  • Website banners
  • Email (for registered users)
